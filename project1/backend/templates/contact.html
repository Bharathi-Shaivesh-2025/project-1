Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the AI layout generation?

Our AI uses statistical analysis and best practices in urban planning to generate recommendations. The layouts are based on proven urban planning principles adapted to your specific inputs. Results should be used as a starting point for detailed planning, not as final designs.

Can I export the generated layout?

Currently, layouts can be viewed and captured. We're working on exporting options for CAD, PDF, and other professional formats in future versions.

What data formats can I use for input?

Our system accepts standard numerical inputs for population and temperature. Weather conditions and road infrastructure are selected from predefined categories for consistency and accuracy.

Is my data secure?

We take data security seriously. All inputs are processed on our secure servers and are not stored permanently unless you specifically choose to save projects. Review our privacy policy for more details.

Can I use this for commercial projects?

Yes! We offer commercial licenses for businesses and government agencies. Contact our enterprise team for pricing and features.

How long does it take to generate a layout?

Most layouts are generated within 5-10 seconds. Complex analysis with large populations may take up to 30 seconds.
