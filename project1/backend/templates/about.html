How Our AI Works

Population Density Analysis

We categorize cities into Low, Medium, High, and Very High density categories based on population figures. This directly influences road width and infrastructure allocation.

Climate Adaptation

Temperature and weather conditions inform our amenity recommendations. Hot climates get cooling centers and water features, while cold areas get community centers. Rainy areas get drainage systems and covered walkways.

Smart Road Planning

Road width is dynamically adjusted from 1 (narrow, quiet streets) to 4 (highways) based on population density. We generate both main roads and secondary roads for optimized traffic flow.

Zone Allocation

The AI intelligently allocates space for different zones:

  • Residential Zones - Housing and family-oriented areas
  • Commercial Zones - Shopping, offices, and businesses
  • Parks & Green Spaces - Essential for livability and environment
  • Industrial Zones - Manufacturing and logistics (for high-density cities)

Amenity Recommendations

Based on population density, climate, and weather, we recommend essential amenities:

  • Parks and recreational spaces
  • Public transit hubs
  • Healthcare facilities
  • Educational institutions
  • Commercial markets
  • Climate-specific facilities
